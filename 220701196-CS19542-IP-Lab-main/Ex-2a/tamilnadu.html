TAMILNADU

Tamil Nadu is the southernmost state of India. The tenth largest Indian state by area and the sixth largest by population, Tamil Nadu is the home of the Tamil people, who speak the Tamil language, one of the longest surviving classical languages and which serves as its official language. The capital and largest city is Chennai.

Tamil is the official language of Tamil Nadu, and the state has a rich cultural heritage. It is known for its classical arts, such as Bharatanatyam and Carnatic music. The Tamil film industry, Kollywood, is also a significant cultural influence.

Tamil Nadu has a diverse economy with strong sectors in agriculture, manufacturing, textiles, automobiles, and information technology.

Tamilnadu is known for:

  • Temples and Architecture: Tamil Nadu is known for its Dravidian-style temples with intricate architecture and carvings.
  • Classical Arts: The state is a hub for classical dance and music especially Bharatanatyam and carnatic music
  • Cuisine: Tamil Nadu offers a diverse and flavorful cuisine. Some popular dishes include:Dosa,Idli.
  • Festivals: Tamilnadu celebrates numerous festivals, including Diwali,Pongal.
